Be stoodius. Form a study group and find study spots specialized to you!

Access your Canvas token:
Log into your UW student account in canvas.uw.edu
Click the “Account” profile picture icon on the left hand panel
Navigate to “Settings” and scroll down to “Approved Integrations”
Click the “+ New Access Token” button
Insert “Stoody study group finder” into the “Purpose” box
Select a date after today’s date
Click “Generate Token” and copy the entire generated token
Paste the generated token into the token box
